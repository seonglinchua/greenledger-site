# GreenLedger Content Outline (v1)

This outline defines the core on-page messaging for the first release of the GreenLedger site.

## 1) Hero
**Goal:** Explain what GreenLedger is in one clear sentence.

- Headline: Sustainable finance, explained with clarity.
- Subhead: Practical guidance for investors, institutions, and businesses navigating transition risk and opportunity.
- CTA: Explore the framework.

## 2) Why it matters
**Goal:** Show business relevance without hype.

- Capital is increasingly priced against climate and transition factors.
- Regulatory and disclosure expectations are expanding.
- Strategy quality now influences cost of capital, reputation, and resilience.

## 3) Core pillars
**Goal:** Present the site’s framework.

- **Materiality first:** Focus on financially relevant sustainability factors.
- **Measurable outcomes:** Prioritize verifiable metrics over promises.
- **Governance discipline:** Define ownership, controls, and review cadence.
- **Long-term value:** Connect climate and social considerations to durable performance.

## 4) Use cases
**Goal:** Help visitors find practical application.

- Investors: Compare issuer readiness and transition credibility.
- Institutions: Build policy-aligned risk frameworks and reporting.
- Businesses: Prioritize initiatives tied to performance and stakeholder trust.

## 5) Insights
**Goal:** Share thought leadership content.

- Short explainers on key sustainable finance topics.
- Commentary on frameworks, disclosures, and market developments.
- Actionable “what this means” summaries.

## 6) Trust / methodology
**Goal:** Explain how conclusions are formed.

- Transparent sourcing and assumptions.
- Clear distinction between fact, interpretation, and scenario.
- Regular content review and updates.

## 7) Contact / CTA
**Goal:** Encourage focused outreach.

- Primary CTA: Request a conversation.
- Secondary CTA: Subscribe for updates.
- Contact block: Email and brief inquiry form (frontend only in v1).
