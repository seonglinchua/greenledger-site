## Copy notes

- Tone: authoritative but accessible; financial professionals are the primary audience but the site should not be jargon-heavy.
- Avoid greenwashing language ("planet-friendly", "eco-conscious"). Stick to finance-native terms (ESG, AUM, disclosure, stewardship).
- Use UK/international English for global audience (e.g. "standardised", "licence").
